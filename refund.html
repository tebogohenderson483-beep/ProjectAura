Refund Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Overview

At Project Aura, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your subscription, we offer a straightforward refund policy.

2. Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a refund if:

  • You request a refund within 30 days of your purchase
  • Your subscription has been active for less than 30 days
  • You have not used more than one refund in a 12-month period
  • You request the refund in writing via our support email

3. Non-Refundable Purchases

The following purchases are non-refundable:

  • Purchases made more than 30 days ago
  • Premium add-ons or one-time purchases
  • Subscriptions that have been cancelled and reactivated

4. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  • Email support@projectaura.com with your request
  • Include your account information and reason for refund
  • We will process your request within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds will be credited to your original payment method

5. Changes to Policy

Project Aura reserves the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting to the website. Your continued use of the service following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about our refund policy, please contact us at support@projectaura.com
