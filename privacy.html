1. Introduction

Project Aura ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Project Aura mobile application and website. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service and the choices you have associated with that data.

Types of Data Collected:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number

Project Aura uses the collected data for various purposes:

The security of your data is important to us but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
